Exercise 4 — Click Enabled Buttons Only

<button>Submit</button>

Challenge 4 — Get Input Fields Where Button = Available

List<WebElement> inputs =
driver.findElements(By.tagName("div"))
      .stream()
      .filter(div -> div.findElement(By.tagName("button"))
                        .getAttribute("name")
                        .equals("Available"))
      .map(div -> div.findElement(By.tagName("input")))
      .toList();
